**Structure:**

1. **Hook** (1-2 sentences) - What makes you unique?

3. **Value** (1 paragraph) - What you do well
4. **Proof** (Bullets) - Key achievements
5. **CTA** (1 sentence) - What you're looking for

### 5. Experience Section

**For Each Role:**

- **Headline:** Clear title (Senior Product Manager, not "PM")
- **Company:** Link to company page
- **Bullets:** Achievements with metrics (not responsibilities)

**Formula:** [Action verb] + [What you did] + [Quantified result]

**Before:**

> Managed the product roadmap and coordinated with engineering

**After:**

> Prioritized 50+ feature requests using RICE, shipping 12 major releases that increased retention from 70% ‚Üí 85%

**Tip:** LinkedIn shows first 3 lines by default ‚Äî make them count!

### 8. Recommendations

**Target:** 3-5 strong recommendations

**Who to Ask:**

- Direct manager (credibility)
- Cross-functional partner (collaboration)
- Direct report (leadership ‚Äî if applicable)
- Stakeholder (impact)

**How to Ask:**

```
Hi [Name],

I'm refreshing my LinkedIn profile and would love a recommendation
from someone who's seen my work up close.

If you're open to it, I'd appreciate 2-3 sentences about [specific
project or skill] we worked on together.

Happy to reciprocate!
```

---

### 9. Activity & Content

**Why Post:**

- LinkedIn algorithm rewards active creators
- Shows thought leadership
- Keeps you top-of-mind for opportunities

**What to Post:**

- Lessons from your work (no confidential info)
- Hot takes on product trends
- Reactions to other content
- Career updates

**Frequency:** 2-4 posts/week for visibility

**Engagement:** Comment on others' posts genuinely

## Networking Best Practices

### Sending Connection Requests

**Template:**

```
Hi [Name],

I came across your profile and was impressed by your work on [specific
thing]. I'm also in [PM/BA] at [company/industry] and would love to
connect and learn from your experience.

[Optional: specific question or common ground]

Best,
[Your name]
```

**Tips:**

- Personalize EVERY request
- Mention something specific from their profile
- Explain why you want to connect
- Keep under 300 characters

---

### Following Up

**After connecting:**

```
Thanks for connecting, [Name]!

I noticed you've worked on [topic] ‚Äî I'd love to hear how you approached
[specific challenge]. Would you be open to a 15-minute chat sometime?

No worries if you're busy ‚Äî appreciate the connection either way!
```
