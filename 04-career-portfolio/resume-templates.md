# Resume Templates

**For PM & BA Roles**

---

## Resume Format Guidelines

### ATS-Friendly Structure

1. **Header** - Name, contact, LinkedIn
2. **Summary** - 2-3 sentences (optional)
3. **Experience** - Reverse chronological
4. **Skills** - Relevant tools and methodologies
5. **Education** - Degrees, certifications

### Key Rules

- ✅ **One page** for <10 years experience
- ✅ **Metrics, metrics, metrics** - Quantify impact
- ✅ **Action verbs** - Led, Launched, Increased, Reduced
- ✅ **Keywords** - Match job description
- ❌ **No "responsibilities included..."** - Focus on achievements
- ❌ **No graphics** for ATS submissions

## Action Verbs by Category

### Leadership

Led, Directed, Managed, Spearheaded, Championed, Orchestrated

### Delivery

Launched, Shipped, Deployed, Released, Delivered, Implemented

### Growth

Increased, Grew, Expanded, Scaled, Accelerated, Boosted

### Efficiency

Reduced, Optimized, Streamlined, Automated, Simplified, Eliminated

### Analysis

Analyzed, Evaluated, Assessed, Measured, Quantified, Investigated

### Collaboration

Collaborated, Partnered, Aligned, Facilitated, Coordinated, United

## Metrics to Include

### PM Metrics

- User growth (MAU, DAU)
- Revenue impact (ARR, MRR, GMV)
- Conversion improvements (%)
- Feature adoption rates
- NPS / CSAT changes
- Churn reduction
- Time-to-market

### BA Metrics

- Process efficiency gains (time, cost)
- Requirements coverage (# of stories)
- Defect detection rate
- Stakeholder satisfaction
- Project delivery (on-time, on-budget)
- ROI of delivered solutions

## Tailoring for Job Descriptions

**Step 1:** Highlight keywords from job posting  
**Step 2:** Mirror exact language where authentic  
**Step 3:** Quantify achievements matching those keywords

**Example:**

- Job says: "Experience with agile methodologies"
- Resume says: "Facilitated 20 sprint planning and retrospective sessions using Scrum framework"
